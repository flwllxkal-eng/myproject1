Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

Introduction

Welcome to our Web App Series. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your privacy. Our commitment is to transparency and the responsible handling of information.

This policy applies to all the web tools and pages available on this website. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal identification information. Our tools, such as the currency converter, unit converter, and case converter, are designed to work entirely within your browser. This means:

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal details.
  • The text you input into our converter tools is processed on your device and is not sent to or stored on our servers.
  • There are no user accounts to create, and you can use our services without any form of registration.

Information We Collect (Non-Personal)

While we don't collect personal data, we do collect anonymous, non-personal information to help us improve our website and services. This includes:

  • Log Data: Like most website operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address (anonymized where possible), browser type, browser version, the pages of our site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages. This data is used for statistical analysis to understand user behavior and improve our site.
  • Cookies: Cookies are small files stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to enhance your experience, for example, to remember your preferences. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our site may not function as intended.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services on our website, such as Google AdSense, to support our site. These third parties have their own privacy policies and may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website.

  • Google AdSense: Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Ads Settings page.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your information. While no online service is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
