Reporting Issues

If you encounter a bug or any other technical issue with one of our tools, please be as detailed as possible in your email. Include the following information to help us resolve the issue quickly:

  • The name of the tool you were using.
  • The browser and device you were using.
  • A description of the issue and the steps to reproduce it.

We appreciate your help in making our tools more reliable for everyone.
