How to Use the Case Converter

Using our Case Converter tool is incredibly simple and designed to be intuitive for everyone. Follow these three easy steps:

  1. Enter Text: Type or paste the text you want to convert into the input box labeled "Enter your text here:". You can input single words, full sentences, paragraphs, or even longer documents.
  2. Choose Case: Select your desired case format. Choose "UPPERCASE" to convert the entire text into capital letters, or "lowercase" to change it all to small letters.
  3. Convert and Copy: Click the "Convert Text" button. Your transformed text will instantly appear in the "Converted text:" box below. You can then click the "Copy to Clipboard" button to easily copy the result for use elsewhere.
